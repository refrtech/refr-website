He decides to list Dessert Bliss on Refr, a marketplace that works on recommendations and digitalizes word-of-mouth marketing.

Listing the Shop on Refr:

Arjun creates a hybrid seller account on Refr and lists his cakes, cupcakes, and other products to be sold both in-store and online. He provides detailed descriptions of each product and the Refr team ensures that his product listings are visually attractive, with high-quality images that showcase the beauty and artistry of his cakes. Refr provides a micro-website wherein all details - shop's location, hours, contact information and social handles are mentioned.

Refr's unique feature of working on recommendations means that satisfied customers will recommend Dessert Bliss to others, thus expanding Arjun's customer base through the power of digital word-of-mouth.
