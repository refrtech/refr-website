    What are Refr Campaigns ?
    Refr campaigns are an advertising solution designed to help small businesses promote their products and services . Refr campaigns lets merchants choose their business goals and needs. These campaigns use smart technology to monitor and improve the performance continuously, so you can spend more time on other business tasks.
    What are the different Campaigns that i can run for my business ?
    You can run multiple campaigns for your business , starting with recommendation campaigns followed by loyalty and offer campaigns which include daily drops and student specific deiunts
    How do I set up the campaigns ?
    After you sign up you will be directed to the campaign page where you can set up your recommendation, loyalty and curated offer campaigns
