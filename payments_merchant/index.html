    FAQâ€™s

    What is Refr Pay?
    Refr Pay allows your customers to pay you through Refr whereby customers can use Refr Tokens to pay , whether its an in-store payment or an online payment .
    How do i accept payments with Refr pay?
    RefrPay transactions are directly reflected in your store wallet customers can pay you in-store or online using RefrPay , these payments are then paid out to your account.
    What is the settlement period?
    For offline vendors the settlement is done on the same day For online stores the settlement is done after the return period is over i.e T+14 days.
    How are payouts processed?
    You need to add/update your Bank account or UPI account details in which you want the amount to be settled . This can be done from the business profile section on the vendor dash. You can then goto the wallets section and click withdraw from the store wallet section.
    What is the commision on every payment transaction?
    Refr charges fees and commission rates to bring value to your business and community. We charge a 2% commission on every transaction.
    What are the fees and taxes to be paid on every payment and payout?
    Card payment and payout processing fees are charged by the payment gateway service provider are levied on every transaction and have to be born by the vendor. Payment processing fees to be paid for payments and payouts , these may vary from between 2-4% depending on the amount transacted. Govt Taxes ie TDS,TCS,GST wherever applicable are levied on every transaction.
    Where can I view my Payments summary?
    All transactions related to payments and payouts can be viewed in the Wallet section of the vendor dash.
    Do i require any documents to be submitted ?
    Yes, you will need to submit your KYC before accepting any kind of payments on Refr.
